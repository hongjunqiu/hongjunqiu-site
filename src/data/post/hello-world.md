Hey there 👋

If you're reading this, welcome to my corner of the internet.

## Who am I?

I'm Hongjun, a product manager based in Shanghai. By day, I build products. By night, I explore AI agents, track markets, and occasionally write about things I find interesting.

## What will I write about?

A few topics I care about:

- **AI & Agents** — I've been deep in the AI agent rabbit hole lately. Building my own personal assistant ([OpenClaw](https://openclaw.ai)) and learning a ton in the process.
- **Product Thinking** — Lessons from building products, making decisions under uncertainty, and navigating the messy reality of shipping things.
- **Markets & Investing** — Not financial advice (obviously), but I find macro trends and market dynamics fascinating.
- **Random Thoughts** — Sometimes I'll just share things I'm thinking about.

## Why write?

Writing helps me think. And if something I share happens to help someone else, that's a bonus.

No posting schedule. No promises. Just thoughts when I have them.
